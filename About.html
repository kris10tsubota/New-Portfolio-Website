ABOUT ME

Hi there! My name is Kristen, and I am a native Californian currently studying International Relations and Management at the University of St Andrews in Scotland.

For those who do not know, art has played a huge part of my life for as long as I can remember. In high school, I developed a deep passion for photography and fine art. While attending Scotland's oldest University, I have further cultivated these skills, with my various involvements in different societies (clubs). During my time here, I have honed my technical skills in design and photography through my various marketing positions on campus. These experiences have helped me discover my love for marketing and advertising. I find it especially enthralling to find new creative ways to spread information or content.
